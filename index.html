About

Behind the work

B.A. Computer Science  •  Minor in Creative Technology & Design

I'm Sophie — a designer and developer who cares about websites that feel intentional, well-crafted, and aligned with the person or brand they're built for. For me, designing and coding go hand-in-hand; having both skill sets means I can fine-tune the smallest details so the final experience matches the original vision, not just a close template.

I have a strong interest in accessibility because technology should grow with people, not leave them behind. I love turning ideas into clear and usable digital spaces, and I especially love the shift that happens when a project moves from "this looks nice" to "this feels like me."
